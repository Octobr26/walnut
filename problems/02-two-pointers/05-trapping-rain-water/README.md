# Trapping Rain Water

        Given bar heights, compute how much rain water is trapped after raining.

        LeetCode: https://leetcode.com/problems/trapping-rain-water/

        ## Examples
        - Input: `height = [0,1,0,2,1,0,1,3,2,1,2,1]`; Output: `6`

        ## Constraints
        - Water above a bar is limited by the lower max wall on either side.
- A two-pointer solution can track both side maximums.
