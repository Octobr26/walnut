# Container With Most Water

        Given vertical line heights, choose two lines that hold the most water with the x-axis. Return the maximum area.

        LeetCode: https://leetcode.com/problems/container-with-most-water/

        ## Examples
        - Input: `height = [1,8,6,2,5,4,8,3,7]`; Output: `49`

        ## Constraints
        - Area is width times the shorter height.
- Moving the shorter side is the useful greedy step.
